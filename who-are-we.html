Who Are We?

The founding of the First Amendment Protection Partnership

Before learning about who the First Amendment Protection Partnership is, its important to underderstand the reasons for founding the partnership in the first place. As most Americans know, the first amendment is one of, if not the, most important amendments to the consitution. If it wasn't for the first amendment, the American people would possibly lose many of the rights we hold today, including the right to freely critize the government and choose what religion to follow.

Our Goal

The First Amendment Protection Partnership may sound like a fancy name or a complicated mission, but in reality we strive for one goal: to stop government imposed restrictions on the first amendment.
