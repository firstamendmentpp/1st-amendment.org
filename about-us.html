About Us

Why does the First Amendment Protection Partnership exist?

The First Amendment Protection Partnership (FAPP) exists to defend your rights to speak, write, and express yourself freely online and offline. We provide resources, guidance, and community support to ensure your voice is never silenced.

What is the First Amendment Protection Partnership's mission?

Our mission is simple: to make the First Amendment accessible and actionable for everyone. Whether it’s understanding your rights, protecting your work online, or navigating legal gray areas, FAPP is here to empower you with knowledge and tools to safeguard your freedom of expression.

How is the First Amendment Protection Partnership different?

Unlike large organizations that operate behind the scenes, FAPP focuses on hands-on, practical guidance for real people—because your right to speak freely is personal, immediate, and non-negotiable.

What does the First Amendment Protection Partnership do?

The First Amendment Protection Partnership serves to do two things: unite and educate people over/about their first amendment rights.

1st-amendment.org is dedicated to promoting free expression, fostering meaningful discussion, and connecting people with resources to defend their First Amendment rights. Our mission is to create a space where individuals can stay informed, share perspectives, and work together to protect the freedoms that define our society.
