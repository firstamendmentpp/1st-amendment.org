What is the First Amendment Protection Partnership?

The First Amendment Protection Partnership is a nonpartisan movement dedicated to defending the First Amendment rights of every American. We exist because freedom of speech is not a privilege — it’s the foundation of democracy — and it's under increasing threat from censorship, surveillance, and government overreach.

What are the First Amendment Protection Partnership's principles?

  1. Nonpartisan, nonviolent, and absolutely focused on free speech.
  2. We defend speech we agree with and speech we hate, because protecting only “popular” speech is not protection at all.
  3. We oppose censorship by government, corporations, or institutions that act as arms of the state.
  4. We welcome anyone — left, right, center, libertarian, apolitical — who shares our commitment to free expression.

Why does the First Amendment Protection Partnership exist?

The First Amendment Protection Partnership was founded to provide a space where people can access, explore, and discuss information freely—without spin, clickbait, or censorship. In an age where headlines dominate attention and opinions often drown out facts, we wanted a site dedicated to giving the First Amendment its full weight: the right to speak, read, and think without restriction. This site is about making it easier to see news, debates, and ideas in context, and to encourage critical thought rather than passive consumption.

1st-amendment.org is dedicated to promoting free expression, fostering meaningful discussion, and connecting people with resources to defend their First Amendment rights. Our mission is to create a space where individuals can stay informed, share perspectives, and work together to protect the freedoms that define our society.
